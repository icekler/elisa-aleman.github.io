Introduction

I am an international PhD student born in Mexico, I have lived in Japan for over 4 years and have gotten used to Japanese language and culture. I am now pursuing research and submitting papers. I received the B.S. and M.S. degree and am currently pursuing a PhD in Information Science and Control Engineering in the Nagaoka University of Technology, Nagaoka, Japan under the instruction of Hirofumi Nonaka. I do research in Artificial Neural Networks, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Mining. My research interests are Text and Data mining for management purposes and multilingual endeavors.
